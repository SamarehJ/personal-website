A note on the room

Why there's AI in this office

The room you just walked through doesn't exist — it was generated by AI. Here's why, honestly.

I'm prototyping: this site will change shape many times, and a generated image lets me rebuild the room as fast as I rethink it. I'm learning these tools, and I learn by making. And I can't yet do the alternative justice — I don't know exactly what I want this place to look like, and I can't afford to pay an artist to make it well. Commissioning someone before I can answer their questions would waste their craft and my money.

So the AI image is scaffolding, not the building. If this room ever settles into its final form, I'd love for human hands to make it real. Until then, you're standing in a sketch — and I'd rather show you a sketch than nothing.

How I think about AI more broadly is a bigger conversation — I call it Creedal AI, and it will have its own place in this room soon.
