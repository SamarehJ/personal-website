Why there's AI in this office

This office doesn't exist — it was generated by AI.

I don't see it as the finished product, but a great way to give you an idea of what I'm envisioning in my mind. As I develop the site, this image and the links will change shape and move around many times.

Given that I can't afford to work with an artist or photographer to make it real, and I don't actually know how I want the final version to appear, I'm taking the opportunity to try out AI image generation. Eventually, when I have a good idea of how I want the final version to look like, and know more about what I want, I'd love for human hands to make it real. Until then, you're standing in a sketch — and I'd rather show you a sketch than nothing.

How I think about AI more broadly is a bigger conversation. Part of it is what I call Creedal AI — see if you can find it in the room.
